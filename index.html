Product
Unsaid is not a template generator. It’s a precision tool for turning inner signals into language — through guided clarification.
Clarify
Ask the questions you didn’t know to ask — until the feeling becomes legible.
From vague emotion → precise meaning.
Compose
Generate wording that matches your intent: apology, closure, gratitude, boundaries — without sounding “AI”.
Voice, tone, distance control.
Reflect
Turn expression into insight. Track patterns in what you couldn’t say — and why.
Memory, meaning, self-model.
Experience
Unsaid feels like a calm conversation that keeps you honest — until you finally recognize what you meant.
Mechanism
It narrows ambiguity via guided prompts, then generates language that preserves nuance (intent, boundaries, warmth, distance).
Differentiation
Most AI writes. Unsaid clarifies first — then writes. That’s the gap between “nice text” and “true language.”
